Rihanna Bursts Into Jimmy Kimmel's Bedroom in April Fools' Prank!

Now that’s some alarm call!

Jimmy Kimmel had the shock of his life in the early hours on April Fools’ Day (Wednesday, April 1) when Rihanna crept into his bedroom and woke him up with a psychedelic performance of “Bitch Better Have My Money!”

The 27-year-old singer was let into his home by Kimmel’s wife, Molly McNearney, and snuck upstairs to his bedroom, where the 47-year-old was sleeping.

Giving the show host a dramatic alarm call, the Barbadian beauty then launched into a hilarious performance of her new hit single, throwing real money across the bed as strobe lights flashed in time with a booming bass.

A bemused Kimmel (who appeared to be naked under his white comforter) woke up and looked to be in total shock as he turned over in bed, pausing only to take out his mouthguard and put it on the bedside table!

“It seemed like a UFO had landed,” the TV star said once he got out of bed and was wandering round his living room in his dressing gown.
