Who Is Biko Wright? Meet The Alone Cast On Instagram

Biko Wright is a contestant on the History’s Alone series. Find out more information about him below!

Biko Wright is an American reality TV star and singer. He is currently one of the finalists of History’s “Alone: Grizzly Mountain” season 8 series, competing to emerge as the winner.

The show “Alone” is a self-documented daily struggle of 10 individuals to survive alone in the wild for as long as possible. The show will is just an episode away from finding its ultimate winner, as the final episode will be broadcasted on August 19.

Alone: Who Is Biko Wright? 

Biko Wright is a band singer and construction laborer from Otis, Oregon.

He is the lead singer of the metal band Sirion and also a part of a folk band, Endelos, that plays at local mead halls, pubs, and festivals in his hometown. 

Biko works as a construction laborer for his daily living. He decided to participate in the “Alone” series this year as he was always a mountain kid in his hometown, Topanga Canyon, California.

He grew up going camping, backpacking, snowboarding, snorkeling, spearfishing or hunting for his family vacations. He has been termed “Mountain Viking” by the Alone series.

Is Biko Wright On Wikipedia? Know His Age 

Biko Wright’s biography has yet to make its debut on Wikipedia. Nevertheless, a brief introduction to his life and his background can be found on the official website of the History channel.

Biko, the Mountain Viking, is currently 30 years old in age as of 2021. Meanwhile, the complete data on his birthday are not on display at the moment.

Being young at 30, he is the most youngest contender in the show this season. The oldest participant of the adventure was Tim Madsen, who was 48 years old when he joined the journey.

However, Tim took his leave from the show just six days after going to the mountains as he suffered from anxiety attacks and chest pains.

Who Are Biko Wright Parents? Know His Background

Biko Wright was inspired by his parents’ adventurous background to look up to adventures and wilderness in life.

His father is a former Los Angeles sheriff’s sergeant and Marine veteran who has a passion for wilderness outings, hunting, and fishing, which he learned from his own father as a child in Fiji.

His father taught him “In a survival situation,” as his life’s constant mantra.

Moreover, his father was the one who taught Biko and his older brother everything about wilderness survival training.

Not to be surprised, Biko trained with his father’s search and rescue team and even participated in avalanche training.

Meet Biko Wright Wife

Biko and his wife share a pair of twin boys from their marriage. While the identity of his wife is not on display, she often appears on Biko’s Instagram posts.

They have been together for more than 6 years, and the couple shares many things in common, including metal rock music.
