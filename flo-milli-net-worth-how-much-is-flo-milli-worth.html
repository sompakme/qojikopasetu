Flo Milli’s Early Influences and Musical Journey

Flo Milli, born Tamia Monique Carter on January 9, 2000, in Mobile, Alabama, developed a strong passion for music from a young age. Influenced by her mother’s favorite artists such as Musiq Soulchild, Anthony Hamilton, Erykah Badu, and Jill Scott, Flo Milli began honing her rap skills during middle school.

In 2015, at the age of 15, Flo Milli released her first solo song titled “No Hook,” showcasing her raw talent and captivating style. It was the beginning of her musical journey that would lead her to greater heights.

An episode of BET’s 106 & Park featuring Nicki Minaj proved to be a pivotal moment for Flo Milli. Inspired by Nicki’s powerful presence and lyrical prowess, Flo Milli started writing verses that would later become the foundation for her own captivating songs.

Despite facing personal challenges like low self-esteem during high school, Flo Milli found solace and empowerment in music. It became her refuge and a means of self-expression, enabling her to develop a unique artistic voice.

Flo Milli actively released her music on platforms like Soundcloud and created her YouTube channel, amassing a growing fan base and building a strong foundation for her career in the music industry. Through her relentless dedication and undeniable talent, Flo Milli laid the groundwork for her future success.

Viral Success with “Beef FloMix” and “In the Party”

One of the defining moments in Flo Milli’s career happened in October 2018 when she recorded “Beef FloMix,” a viral reimagining of Playboi Carti and Ethereal’s song “Beef.” This track gained popularity on Instagram and TikTok, reaching the number 2 spot on Spotify’s viral 50 in April 2019. Building on this success, she released another hit single, “In the Party,” in October 2019. These milestones showcased Flo Milli’s growing popularity and led to her performance at Rolling Loud Los Angeles in December of the same year.

Record Deals and Major Releases

Flo Milli’s undeniable talent and unique style caught the attention of major record labels, catapulting her to new heights in the music industry. She signed with RCA Records and ’94 sounds, opening doors for her to release a series of successful singles that showcased her magnetic presence and lyrical prowess.

Some of Flo Milli’s standout tracks include:

  • “My Attitude”: A high-energy anthem that showcases her unapologetic attitude and infectious confidence.
  • “Not Friendly”: A fiery track where Flo Milli asserts her dominance and sends a clear message to her haters.
  • “Eat It Up”: A bouncy and playful song that demonstrates her versatility as an artist.

In July 2020, Flo Milli dropped her highly anticipated debut mixtape, “Ho, Why Is You Here?”. The mixtape made waves in the industry and debuted at an impressive #78 on the Billboard 200 chart, solidifying her position as one of hip-hop’s rising stars.

Continuing her momentum, Flo Milli earned a nomination for Best New Artist at the 2020 BET Hip Hop Awards, further cementing her status as a force to be reckoned with. And in July 2022, she released her debut album, “You Still Here, Ho?”, which further showcased her growth as an artist and expanded her discography.

With each release, Flo Milli continues to captivate audiences with her distinct style and charismatic presence. Her music resonates with fans and has solidified her status as a rising star in the rap industry.
