Quavo Goes Viral After Getting Sexual Advances On Dating Show

Quavo has gone viral after a clip of him receiving sexual advances from a contestant on a dating show surfaced on social media.

The Atlanta rapper, who recently appeared on popular YouTube personality Noluvmar’s 20 Women Vs 1 Rapper series, was seemingly caught off guard when one woman, Riley from Kentucky, listed having sexual intercourse and performing fellatio as two of her favorite activities.

During the final round of the competition, Quavo asked Riley what she likes to do, to which she responds by saying she “likes to have fun.” This led the rapper to inquire what type of “fun” the contestant likes to have and if she’s “adventurous,” with Riley responding by saying that she likes “traveling, f**king, sucking.”

Visibly bashful in light of her response, Quavo began to stammer while asking if she has any other adventurous activities she likes to engage in, such as “riding a horse.” The rapper than said that he’s unsure if he could “eat at a table” with the contestant due to her overt sexual innuendo.

At the end of the competition, Quavo chose another contestant named Trinity, a fellow Atlanta native, as the winner of the competition. Riley finished among the last five women left in the competition, but enjoyed what many would deem as the most viral and memorable moment of all of the contestants on the series.

Other rap artists that have previously appeared on 20 Women Vs 1 Rapper include DaBaby and NLE Choppa.
