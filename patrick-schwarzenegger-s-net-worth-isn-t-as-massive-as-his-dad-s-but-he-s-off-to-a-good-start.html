Patrick Schwarzenegger's Net Worth Isn't As Massive As His Dad's, But He's Off To A Good Start

Highlights

  • Patrick Schwarzenegger is building his own career in Hollywood, separate from his famous parents, by pursuing different projects and roles.
  • Despite coming from a wealthy family, Patrick is actively working to build his own net worth, which is currently around $8 million.
  • In addition to his acting career, Patrick is involved in various business ventures, including modeling, real estate, and a shared business venture with his mom.

The most famous Schwarzenegger name is, of course, Arnold's, but now that his kids are all grown, they crop up in the media on occasion. Patrick Schwarzenegger appears more often than his siblings thanks to his career path, which while similar to his dad's is also something entirely new.

Rather than going for action films or movie series with long-term potential, Patrick seems to be dabbling in various projects and building up his portfolio. So far, it seems to be working well, as Patrick Schwarzenegger's net worth is already impressive for someone who, thanks to family money, probably never had to work if he didn't want to.

Patrick Is More Than Just Arnold Schwarzenegger's Son

Most biographies of Patrick Schwarzenegger start out by describing him as the son of Arnold Schwarzenegger and Maria Shriver. While that's true, Patrick hasn't relied on his parents' fame to build his career in Hollywood. At least, it doesn't appear that way.

Rather than follow in his father's Terminator footsteps, Patrick started working in Hollywood with very different gigs. His acting debut was in 2006, when he was about 13, when he was a random "jock kid" in The Benchwarmers.

After that, things were quiet for Patrick until his next gig in 2012, where he played a minor character in the Kristen Bell-supported Stuck in Love. The film also featured Greg Kinnear, Jennifer Connelly, and Lily Collins.

Next, Patrick appeared in an Ariana Grande music video, of all things, and then started appearing in other projects like a zombie apocalypse film and the TV series Scream Queens. More recently, Patrick has landed more significant gigs, including eight episodes each on shows like The Long Road Home and The Staircase and a recurring gig on The Terminal List (with his brother-in-law Chris Pratt).

In 2018, Patrick starred alongside Bella Thorne in the movie Midnight Sun; it wasn't exceptionally well-received by viewers, but working with Bella, who was previously a Disney darling and is still a big name today, might have given Patrick's resume a boost.

Now, perhaps his most anticipated gig is as Golden Boy, a superhero from the world of The Boys, in Gen V. A September 2023 series premiere on Amazon Prime could give Patrick the visibility he needs to build an impressive career in Hollywood.

To date, of course, his gigs have been relatively minor, as evidenced by his current net worth.

Patrick Schwarzenegger's Net Worth Is About $8 Million

As of 2023, Patrick Schwarzenegger's net worth is said to be $8 million. That might seem like pennies for the son of famed Terminator Arnold Schwarzenegger, whose net worth hit $450 million and was still rising going into 2023.

Patrick might be in line for some family money, and he probably didn't have to get a regular job, as most celebrity kids don't, but he's working on building a career all his own. And while not all of Arnold's other kids' net worths are public, Patrick may have the highest of them all.

Even Katherine Schwarzenegger Pratt, on her own, is worth less than her little brother at $3 million. She's married to multi-millionaire Chris Pratt, of course, but on her own, Katherine hasn't built up quite as illustrious a career just yet.

But despite Patrick's successes in Hollywood and regarding his finances, it's unclear what kind of price his acting fetches, and whether the family name helps or hurts. Patrick doesn't seem to be looking for Arnold-inspired gigs, as evidenced by his appearance in a teen romance movie and various TV shows lacking any robotic mercenaries.

Patrick Schwarzenegger Runs Various Businesses

Patrick has also picked up some side gigs, including one with his mom. In a March 2023 Instagram post, Patrick and his mom Maria appeared in a promotional video for Mosh Life, their shared business venture.

The business was relatively new when Patrick and Maria shared that their product had made it onto store shelves, but it's not the only thing Patrick has been working on.

He also models, including for Hugo Boss and other brands, and has appeared in various magazines as both a model and interview subject. Modeling seemingly came naturally for Patrick, who even launched his own clothing line as a teen.

While that arguably required some investment from his parents, Patrick doesn't seem content to sit back and rely on his family's money or fame to get him work. He also dabbles in real estate, buying homes, working on them a bit, and then reselling.

His real estate margins weren't massive, but Patrick seems to enjoy working on properties, and it's unclear whether he currently owns a home or possibly rents. What is known is that he lives with his long-time girlfriend, model Abby Champion.

Abby features in Patrick's Instagram posts, and though the two haven't announced an engagement, they seem very serious and have been since 2016.
