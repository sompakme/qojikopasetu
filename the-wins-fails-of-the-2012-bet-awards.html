The Wins &amp; Fails Of The 2012 BET Awards

BET claimed that its 2012 Awards Show would be “too big to miss.” And indeed it was no epic fail. It was worth tuning in just for the reactions from the front row—where Jay-Z, Beyoncé, Ye and his boo Kim Kardashian were seated—which produced more gifs than Santa. Here, we recap the most cry-able and laughable moments.

WINS

The return of D’Angelo, Afro and all
Jay-Z FINALLY joining the Kanye/Taylor Swift “I’ma let you finish” mockery
Nicki Minaj’s bootylicious performance revenge
Beyoncé rapping along to “Beez in the Trap”
Kanye’s mic-drop after “New God Flow”
Slaughterhouse’s anti-ratchay Shakespearean presentation
Battle of the cakes: Nicki Minaj vs. Elle Varner
