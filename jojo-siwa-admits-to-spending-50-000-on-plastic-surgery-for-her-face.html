In a rediscovered video clip featuring the pop star talking to Buzzfeed, JoJo disclosed that the most expensive item she wore to a recent awards ceremony was her teeth. When she elaborated on her dental work, she referred to her veneers by humorously stating, "These motherf–kers cost me 50 grand."

Veneers are thin, custom-made shells crafted from tooth-colored materials, such as porcelain or composite resin. They are designed to cover the front surface of teeth to improve their appearance, addressing issues like discoloration, unevenness, or chips. The procedure typically involves removing a small amount of enamel from the tooth's surface to accommodate the veneer's thickness.

As for cost, veneers can vary widely depending on factors like material, location, and the dentist's expertise. On average, porcelain veneers can range from $1,000 to $2,500 per tooth, while composite veneers may be slightly less expensive, ranging from $500 to $1,500 per tooth. People tend to have 32 teeth per total, which explains how JoJo was able to rack up a $50,000 bill for her smile alone.

JoJo’s veneers may seem expensive, but she’s one of few celebrities to not only admit to cosmetic enhancements, but to name the price paid for it. The singer-actress hasn’t disclosed if she’s had nay more work done.

With a net worth of $20 million, the $50,000 price tag may have seemed like a small investment for JoJo, especially since her face has become her brand.
