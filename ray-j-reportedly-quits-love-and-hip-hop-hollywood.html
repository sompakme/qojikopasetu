Ray J Reportedly Quits 'Love And Hip Hop Hollywood'

Ray J has recently decided to drop out of Love & Hip-Hop Hollywood, in efforts to save his relationship with Princess Love. This decision was made on Monday (Mar. 23) according to TMZ .

After the first season ended, the 34-year-old and Love reconciled, but their relationship still suffered from some major setbacks. Love was arrested back in February in New Orleans for domestic violence. You may also recall the infamous argument where Princess threw a glass of wine at Ray, and later fell into a swimming pool. Back in February, the two were also reportedly involved in a violent struggle.

Before walking away from LHHH, Ray said he would be willing to shoot scenes without any fights or arguments in reality TV fashion, but the producers did not go for the deal.

The producers are set to be meeting with him today (Mar. 25) to try and see if he will stay on the show, reports TMZ. – Richy Rosario
