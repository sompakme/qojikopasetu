This Weeks WWE RAW Viewership Information; Did Shane &amp; Undertaker Help Draw Viewers?

According to ShowBuzz Daily, this week’s WWE RAW viewership information saw the March 14th episode of RAW bring in 3.457 million viewers, which is down from last week’s 3.551 million viewers.

This week’s show, featuring appearances by Shane McMahon, Vince McMahon and the Undertaker, saw an hourly breakdown of 3.471 million viewers in the first hour, 3.392 million viewers in the second hour, and the final hour brought in 3.510 million viewers. The third hour rise was notable since RAW’s final hour had seen a drop in viewers between the second and third hours over the past several weeks. 

Overall, WWE RAW ranked second in total viewership for the night, and first overall in the 18-49 key demographic.
