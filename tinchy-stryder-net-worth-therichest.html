Musician Tinchy Stryder has an estimated net worth of $4 million. Stryder was born Kwasi Danquah III in Accra, Ghana, and holds both British and Ghanaian nationality. He was born on the 14th of September 1986 and has released three solo studio albums. Most noted for being a rapper, he also has other notable achievements such as being a music executive, businessman, and A&R executive.

Danquah attended St Bonaventures' Catholic Comprehensive School and has a degree in Digital Arts, Moving Image and Animation which was gained from the University of East London. His early exposure in the music industry is noted by appearing on pirate radio stations, developing his rapping skills and battling other non-mainstream artists. His role in the grime group Roll Deep gave him exposure to work with other rappers and make a name for himself.

Other ventures include various businesses such as Star in the Hood which is a clothing brand.
