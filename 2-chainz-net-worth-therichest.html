Born Tauheed Epps on September 12, 1977 in College Park Georgia, 2 Chainz is a hip hop recording artist with an estimated net worth amounting to $2 million.

On his early years in the industry, he is known to be a part of the Playaz Circle who is best recognized for the single titled Duffle Bag Boy. Currently, 2 Chainz works for Def Jam Records which is just an imprint of the Universal Music Group.
