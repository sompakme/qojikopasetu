Jeff Hardy is a professional wrestler. He is also a painter, musician, songwriter-singer and a poet. Jeff’s estimated net worth stands at an impressive $12 Million. His wealth comes from being a part of the World Wrestling Federation/Entertainment.

At present, he is signed to Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA). At TNA, he is the reigning TNA World Heavyweight Champion in his third reign. He is well known for his stint with the WWE. WWE described Hardy as "one of the most popular WWE Champions in history”.

Jeff Hardy was born on 31st August, 1977 in Cameron, North Carolina, United States. He tried different kinds of sports and finally decided to make a career in wrestling. He has won many awards for being one of the best wrestlers. Some of the awards include the National Championship Light Heavyweight Championship-won two times, the New Dimension Wrestling Light Heavyweight Championship that he won once, the NDW Tag Team Championship with Matt Hardy, the NFWA Heavyweight Championship, and the World Heavyweight Championship that he has won twice, among others.

Jeff Hardy has many tattoos on his body. One of them starts from his head and ends in his hand. He was in a relationship with Beth Britt since 1999 and got married to her in 2011.

He was once not allowed to board the flight in Nashville International Airport as the Southwest Airline employees reported that Hardy was intoxicated. He was even arrested because of carrying anabolic steroids and cocaine. It was found that Hardy was trafficking controlled prescription drugs. He spent 10 days in jail, and 30 months of probation. He was even charged a $100,000 fine.
