Kim Kardashian Shows Post-Baby Body in Skinny Jeans, Sweater: Picture

Kim Kardashian is back on neutral ground, sartorially speaking. One day after going glam for boyfriend Kanye West's headline-making visit to Jimmy Kimmel Live, the 32-year-old reality star opted for a more casual, low-key look.

Stepping out in Beverly Hills on Thursday, Oct. 10, to take daughter North, 3 months, to the doctor, the fashion-loving first-time mom showed off her slim post-baby bod in pale, blush-colored skinny jeans and a matching, slightly blousy sweater. Her feet — once perpetually squeezed into sky-high stilettos — were clad in nude flat sandals.

The dressed-down look is in keeping with Kardashian's laid-back street style since giving birth to baby North in June. But there have been glimmers of her past, pre-pregnancy wardrobe in recent weeks — first in Paris, where she rocked several revealing, form-fitting frocks, and then at Jimmy Kimmel's studios on Wednesday, Oct. 9.
