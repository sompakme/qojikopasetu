John Mayer Gets Trolled In Recent IG Post By Katy Perry's Rumored Ex, Ryan Phillippe

Singer John Mayer has been quiet in the music world, but that does not make him inactive in his passion or being present on social media. Aside from his career as an artist, he is linked to many female celebrities he has dated over the years, including Jennifer Aniston, Taylor Swift, and arguably his most publicized one, Katy Perry.

On Instagram, John has shared his promotion with Land Rover USA with beautiful scenery photos of him traveling with his new ride. This would have been just a normal promotional post doing something he enjoys, but the rumored ex-partner of Katy Perry, Ryan Phillippe replied to John's Instagram post, invoking curiosity amongst fans.
