Who Is Helene Fischer?, Helene Fischer Biography and Wiki

Helene Fischer is a German singer, dancer, entertainer, television presenter, and actress who was born in the Siberian city of Krasnoyarsk, Russia. Since her debut in the year 2005 she has won numerous awards, including seventeen Echo awards, four “Die Krone der Volksmusik” awards as well as three Bambi awards. Fischer has sold at least 15 million records according to record certifications.

Her multi-platinum 2013 album Farbenspiel became the most legally-downloaded album ever by a German artist in June 2014 and is now the sixth-bestselling album of all time in Germany. Fischer’s signature song “Atemlos durch die Nacht” was the bestselling song in Germany in the year 2014.

She has had the best-selling album of the year in Germany 5 times, in 2013, 2014, 2015, 2017, and 2018. She was ranked number 8 on Forbes list of The World’s Highest-Paid Women In Music in the year 2018, earning 32 million dollars. Fischer is best known as the “Queen of Schlager”.

Helene Fischer Education

Fischer joined the Frankfurt Stage & Musical School for 3 years, where she studied singing and acting after graduating from school. During this time, she performed on stage at the Staatstheater Darmstadt and on the stage of the Volkstheater Frankfurt.

Helene Fischer Parents and Sister

Helene Fischer was born to Peter Fischer(father) and Marina Fischerin(mother) in the Siberian city of Krasnoyarsk, Russia (then the Soviet Union). Her father served as a physical education teacher while her mother as an engineer.

Her paternal grandparents were Black Sea Germans who were among those sent to forced settlements in Kazakhstan and Siberia. When she was three and a half years of age, she moved alongside her parents and her six-year older sister Erica to Wöllstein, Rhineland-Palatine, West Germany in 1988.

Helene Fischer Career

During her time at the Stage & Musical School, her mother secretly copied a demo CD with six of Fischer’s songs and sent it to different recording studios to gauge the reaction of professionals. Fischer’s debut on stage took place on 14 May 2005, in a program of the TV channel ARD. It was a duet with the singer Florian Silbereisen. The two later became a couple.

She has won sixteen “Echo” awards, the “Goldene Henne” award six times, and the “Krone der Volksmusik” prize four times. All her albums and DVDs have achieved multiple gold and double platinum status. Her songs, with their stories of the everyday worries and woes of home-loving folks, are lyrically close to what could be called “country music” although musically quite different. Her fanbase extends beyond Germany, Austria, and Switzerland to Belgium, the Netherlands, Denmark, Norway, the United Kingdom, Finland, the United States, and New Zealand.

On 7 June 2010, she released her first English album, “The English Ones”. The album was produced by Jean Frankfurter , a German producer, composer, and arranger for 40 years. On singing in English, Fischer commented: “I have always dreamed of singing my songs in another language. English is the language of Country, the music that we call ’Schlager’. Language is a tool, but it is important that what you sing comes from the heart – and that is what this album is about.”In January 2013 she made her acting debut in an episode of the German TV series Das Traumschiff.

She released her album “Farbenspiel” in Germany on 4 October 2013. In 2014, she played a tour and a big stadium tour in 2015. The album received platinum after 5 days in Germany, Denmark, and Austria. Farbenspiel is the most successful release of a German female artist in the first week of release in the last 10 years. After selling over 2,000,000 copies of her last album, a new album was announced for May 2017 which has already been pre-ordered 400,000 times.

Helene Fischer 2018
