Travis Kelces Ex-Girlfriend Maya Benberry Cryptically Warns Taylor Swift That Hes A Cheater

Highlights

  • Travis Kelce's ex-girlfriend warns Taylor Swift to be smart and implies that Travis may be using Taylor for fame.
  • Travis' connection to Taylor has increased his jersey sales and put him in the headlines, possibly benefiting his career.
  • Travis has a history of infidelity in relationships, as Maya suggests he cheated on her with another woman during their relationship.

While many fans believe Taylor Swift has found a gem in her new love interest Travis Kelce, the NFL star’s ex-girlfriend recently had a very public warning for the singer.

Maya Benberry, who dated Travis in 2016 after winning his E! dating reality series Catching Kelce, recently opened up to the Daily Mail about what led to their split.

Travis Kelce’s Ex Claims He’s A Cheater Who’s Using Taylor For Fame

“Taylor seems like such a fun girl with a beautiful spirit so I wish her the best of luck but I wouldn’t be a girls' girl if I didn’t advise her to be smart!” Maya told the outlet.

The marketing professional also implied that Travis may be using Taylor for fame – it’s been reported that Travis’ jersey sales have skyrocketed since Taylor was spotted attending his Chiefs vs. Bears game. "Yesterday, Travis Kelce was one of the top 5 selling NFL players and saw a nearly 400% spike in sales throughout the Fanatics network of sites, including NFLShop.com," Fanatics explained to TMZ.

Needless to say, Travis’ recent connection to Taylor has put him directly in the headlines and will continue to do so as long as he’s linked to her, which he may see as a good move for his career. “I’m sure by now she has mastered the ability to see who is really there for her - and who is just using her,” Maya continued. “I can only hope that she comes out the teacher in this situation and not the student.”

“The man that I knew him to be when we dated,” she went on. “Only time will tell but like the saying always goes, once a cheater, always a cheater.”

Travis Kelce Has A History Of Infidelity In Relationships

Maya and Travis only dated for two months after she came first place on Catching Kelce, where she joined 49 other contestants representing all 50 states in vying for the affection and love of the NFL player. As Daily Mail notes, Maya posted a series of now-deleted tweets following their 2016 break-up in which she suggested that Travis had cheated on her with sports broadcaster Kayla Nicole.

“When you and your ex broke up five months ago but you find out via social media that he’s supposedly been in another relationship for 6,” she wrote a the time.

Travis went on to date Maya from 2017 to 2022, though the long-time couple faced allegations of infidelity in 2020 when they were on a break.

According to PEOPLE, the pair are still getting to know each other to see where their romantic connection takes them. "They're having fun," a source told the outlet. "This was Taylor's first time meeting his mom and dad, and everyone was enjoying themselves. She was super chill and low-key hanging out with his friends and family."

Taylor has been linked to several famous men, including controversial musician Matty Healy, in the wake of her break-up from long-time boyfriend Joe Alywn earlier this year.
