The Five Nights At Freddy's Cast, Ranked By Net Worth

Highlights

  • The horror video game Five Nights At Freddy's has expanded into a successful franchise, including subsidiary games, books, and a movie adaptation released in 2023.
  • The cast members of the Five Nights At Freddy's movie were carefully chosen for their previous accomplishments in cinema, and each brought unique qualities to their character roles.
  • The estimated net worth of the cast members varies, with the highest being Cory DeVante Williams at $100 million, making him the wealthiest voice actor and among the cast members.

The acclaimed horror video game Five Nights At Freddy's has risen to an incredible level of fame since it was first introduced.

The brand expanded remarkably over time, culminating in a variety of subsidiary games and a sequence of books that delve deeper into the enigmatic universe of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. The prosperity of this franchise extends beyond just gaming and literature; it has also made its way into cinematic world.

In 2023, the eagerly awaited movie adaptation finally hit screens, bringing the chilling animatronic characters to life. The film showcases a skilled group who lend their talents to bring life to the eerie characters within Freddy Fazbear's Pizza.

These cast members were meticulously selected for their previous accomplishments in cinema, and tasked with infusing unique qualities into each character role they portrayed.

In this list, we examine each Five Nights At Freddy's actor’s financial status as of 2023.

10 Roger Joseph Manning Jr. (As Foxy)

$2 Million

The actor known as Roger Joseph Manning Jr., who lent his voice to the character Foxy, has an estimated net worth of $2 million.

Manning Jr., a multi-faceted musician and songwriter, has been part of bands like Jellyfish, the Moog Cookbook, and Imperial Drag. His character Foxy is an antagonistic, pirate-themed robotic fox in both the game and film interpretations.

Manning Jr. not only possesses singing abilities but also a wide array of instrumental skills such as piano, guitar, bass and percussion.

8 Jade Kindar-Martin (As Bonnie)

$5 Million

Jade Kindar-Martin, a stunt performer and high-wire artist with experience in shows such asAmerica's Got Talent, Cirque du Soleil and films like The Walk, takes on the role of Bonnie – an animatronic bunny who plays guitar while being fond of lurking within dark corners.

His net worth is estimated to be around $5 million. Kindar-Martin holds two Guinness World Record titles; one for the longest high-wire walk and another for the fastest crossing of a kilometer-long wire situated over South Korea's Han River 456.

Kindar-Martin made his vows to fellow stunt performer Karine Mauffrey on a wire stretched above France's Château de Chantilly. He belongs to The Flying Wallendas, which is a well-known family engaged in performing stunts like the "Seven-Man Pyramid."

3 Matthew Lillard (As William Afton)

$40 Million
