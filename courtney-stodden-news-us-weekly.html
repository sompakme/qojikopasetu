Courtney Stodden News - Us Weekly

Name:Courtney Stodden

Hometown:Tacoma, Washington

Relationship:Single

Birthday:August 29, 1994

About

The youngest of three siblings, Courtney Stodden who uses they/them pronouns, was born on August 29, 1994. In 2010, they won the Miss Teen Washington USA pageant, which catapulted their modeling career. They then signed up for an acting workshop, which was taught by Doug Hutchison. The pair began dating despite their 34-year age gap.

In May 2011, Stodden, then 16, married Hutchison in Las Vegas. The following year, the couple were featured on season 2 of VH1’s Couples Therapy. After nearly separating in 2013, they renewed their vows on their fifth anniversary.

Stodden announced in May 2016 that the couple were expecting their first child together. However, the model suffered a miscarriage in July of that year.

The reality star confirmed their split from Hutchison in February 2017. They filed for divorce in March 2018, and the proceedings were finalized in March 2020.

Stodden was linked to Brian Austin Green in June 2020. The actor distanced himself from the TV personality the following month after they posted a bizarre video of the pair on Instagram amid his budding relationship with model Tina Louise.

In April 2021, Stodden came out as non-binary.
