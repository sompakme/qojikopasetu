What is the most underrated Big Brother moment?

Since Big Brother 5 aired in 2005, a fantastic moment from the season has been mostly forgotten which is why it is so underrated now. During a truly touching moment, Michael "Cowboy" Ellis and Nakomis Dedmon came to a startling realization. The duo who seemed wildly different at first glance turned out to be half-siblings as they realized they shared a father without knowing it. Since the pair were both such lovable people, watching them realize their true relationship was a joy to behold.
