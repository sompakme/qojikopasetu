Damien Williams Contract, Salary, Net Worth, College, Height

Damien Williams is an American football running back for the Chicago Bears of the National Football League (NFL) who was born April 3, 1992. He played college football in Oklahoma. He was signed by the Miami Dolphins as an undrafted free agent in 2014.

He has also played for the Kansas City Chiefs, and won Super Bowl LIV with the team. In that Super Bowl, he scored the Chiefs’ game-clinching touchdown on a 38-yard run with less than two minutes left in the game.

Damien Williams College

Williams was originally expected to play college football at Arizona State but did not meet the ACT test score requirement. So he played at Arizona Western College before transferring to the University of Oklahoma in 2012. In November 2013, he was dismissed from Oklahoma after violating team rules. In two seasons with the Sooners, he rushed for 1,499 yards with 18 touchdowns, including a 95-yard touchdown run against Texas in 2012.
