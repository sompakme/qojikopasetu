Fans Mistake Madonna's Boyfriend For Jaden Smith

Madonna has been dating Ahlamalik Williams since the summer of 2019, and their relationship seems to be going strong in spite of their 35-year age gap.

People feel really badly for the Smith family right now, as he mistakenly got dragged for being intimately linked to Madonna.

Ahlamalik Looks Like Jaden Smith
