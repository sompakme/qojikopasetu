Ryan Leslie Offers $1 Million For Return Of Stolen Laptop

Desperate times call for expensive measures. 

According to NY Daily News, Ryan Leslie is offering $1 million to anyone who will return his stolen Macbook to him. The singer made an initial offer of $20,000 when his laptop was stolen from inside a black Mercedes while on tour in Germany October 23. But as the days rolled sans his computer—and music— Leslie decided to up the dollar signs.

“For the record, I had so many amazing music & visual projects on that MacBook that I was working on to share with u this winter,” the singer tweeted to his more than 200,000 followers Saturday. 

The Harlemite claims he left a bag containing his MacBook in the back seat of a secured Mercedes. Security drivers briefly left the car unattended to escort him into the nightclub and when Leslie returned, the bag was gone. In addition to his laptop, the bag also contained $10,000 cash and his passport. 

Leslie’s asked that if anyone has information regarding his stolen laptop to please email ryanleslielaptopreturn@gmail.com.
