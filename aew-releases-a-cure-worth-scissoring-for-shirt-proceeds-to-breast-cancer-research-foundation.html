AEW Releases A Cure Worth Scissoring For Shirt, Proceeds To Breast Cancer Research Foundation

AEW is giving fans something worth scissoring for.

AEW star Anthony Bowens of The Acclaimed took to Twitter to promote the team’s new shirt. Named “A Cure Worth Scissoring For,” the shirt is in support of Breast Cancer Awareness Month. 100% of royalties will be donated to the Breast Cancer Research Foundation.

The listing for the shirt notes that 100% of royalties will be donated to the Breast Cancer Research Foundation.

Bowens and his tag team partner, Max Caster, have turned scissoring into one of the most popular taunts in the wrestling world. The duo, alongside “Daddy Ass” Billy Gunn, holds the AEW World Trios Championship. They will defend the gold at AEW WrestleDream: Zero Hour.

WrestleZone will have coverage of the show as it airs.
