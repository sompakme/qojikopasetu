Positions: These Are Ariana Grande's Top 10 Billboard Hits

In the vast landscape of the music industry, few artists have captured the hearts of listeners. Ariana Grande with her powerhouse vocals, infectious melodies, and undeniable talent, has become a global sensation. From her early days as a Nickelodeon star to her current status as a pop star, she has consistently delivered chart-topping hits that have left a significant mark on the music industry. Ariana Grande's journey to stardom began with her breakout role on the Nickelodeon series Victorious. However, her transition from television to music truly showcased her immense talent. With her debut single, The Way, featuring Mac Miller, Grande made an unforgettable entrance into the music scene.

Grande's discography boasts six studio albums, each showcasing her evolution as an artist. From the sweet melodies of Yours Truly to the empowering anthems of Thank U, Next, and Positions, Grande has consistently pushed boundaries and defied expectations.

Positions - 1 Million

In 2020, Ariana Grande took the world by storm with her sultry and empowering track, Positions. Serving as the title track of her sixth studio album of the same name, this mesmerizing song debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 and maintained the position for a week, according to Billboard. Positions exuded a seductive R&B sound and captivating lyrics propelling it to sell over a million copies nationwide.

Side To Side Feat. Nicki Minaj - 1.1 Million Copies

Released in 2016, Side to Side is a collaboration between Ariana Grande and Nicki Minaj. According to Official Charts, this infectious track became an instant hit, peaking at No. 4 on the Billboard Hot 100. With its reggae-infused sound and empowering lyrics, Side to Side sold over 1.1 Million copies and showcased Grande's ability to create music that effortlessly blends genres and captivates listeners.

Thank U, Next - 2 Million Copies

Released in 2018, this empowering anthem debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 and spent an impressive seven weeks at the top spot. With its catchy chorus and honest lyrics, Thank U, Next resonated with listeners worldwide and sold over 2 Million copies, further solidifying Grande's status as a pop sensation. The song's music video, which paid homage to popular teen movies, broke the record for the most-watched music video within 24 hours on YouTube at the time of its release.

34+35 Feat. Doja Cat & Megan Thee Stallion - 2 Million Copies

Prepare to groove to the irresistible beats of 34+35, a collaboration between Ariana Grande, Doja Cat, and Megan Thee Stallion. Released in 2021, this sizzling track quickly climbed the charts, reaching No. 2 on the Billboard Hot 100. With its playful lyrics and infectious chorus, 34+35 was certified double platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) and sold over 2 million copies.

Save Your Tears With The Weeknd - 3 Million Copies

Released in 2021, Save Your Tears is a celebrated collaboration between Ariana Grande and The Weeknd. This mesmerizing track reached the top spot on the Billboard Hot 100, captivating listeners with its captivating vocals and atmospheric production. Save Your Tears perfectly embodied Grande's ability to create mesmerizing pop ballads and sold over 3 Million copies.

No Tears Left To Cry - 3 Million Copies

In 2018, Ariana Grande returned triumphantly to the music scene with No Tears Left to Cry. This empowering anthem was a testament to her resilience and strength in adversity. Debuted at No. 3 on the Billboard Hot 100, the song's infectious melody and uplifting lyrics struck a chord with listeners worldwide. No Tears Left to Cry sold over three million copies in the US and solidified Grande's status as a pop icon.

Bang Bang With Jessie J & Nicki Minaj - 3.5 Million Copies

In 2014, Ariana Grande joined forces with Jessie J and Nicki Minaj to deliver the explosive hit Bang Bang. This high-energy collaboration quickly became a fan favorite, reaching No. 3 on the Billboard Hot 100. Owing to its infectious hooks and powerhouse vocals, Bang Bang bagged a Grammy Nomination for Best Pop Duo/Group Performance. It reached the top ten in multiple other countries, including the UK, Belgium, Bulgaria, Canada, and Denmark, to name a few.

Problem Feat. Iggy Azalea - 3.7 Million Copies

Problems featuring Iggy Azalea marked a defining moment in Ariana Grande's career. Released in 2014, this infectious track quickly climbed the charts, peaking at No. 2 on the Billboard Hot 100 and selling over 438,000 copies within a week, as per Wikiwand. Problem remained on the Hot 100 for Sixteen Weeks and became one of Grande's signature songs.

Die For You With The Weeknd - 5 Million Copies

One of Ariana Grande's recent collaborations, Die For You with The Weeknd, has taken the music world by storm. Released in 2023, this stunning track quickly ascended to the top spot on the Billboard Hot 100, showcasing the undeniable chemistry between these two musical powerhouses. With hauntingly beautiful vocals and captivating production, Die For You remained at the top of the Hot 100 for a week. It showcased Grande's ability to create music that resonates deeply with listeners.

7 Rings - 5 Million Copies

7 Rings is one of Ariana Grande's biggest hits to date. Released on January 18, 2019, the song quickly gained popularity and debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. It remained at the top spot for eight consecutive weeks and sold over 5 Million copies in the US, making it one of Grande's longest-running No. 1 hits. The song's success can be attributed to its catchy melody, confident lyrics, and the way it resonated with listeners.

Ariana Grande's journey in the music industry has been nothing short of extraordinary. She has consistently delivered chart-topping hits that have resonated with audiences worldwide. Grande’s ability to blend genres, collaborate with other talented artists, and create infectious melodies has solidified her status as one of the biggest pop stars in the industry.
