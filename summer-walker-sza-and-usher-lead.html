Summer Walker, SZA, And Usher Lead

BET has announced the 2023 Soul Train Awards nominees, with SZA, Usher, and Summer Walker leading the way with nine nods each, making history with a three-way tie. 21 Savage follows with eight nominations while Coco Jones and Victoria Monét tie with six nominations.

According to the network, the nominees were chosen from “a voting academy of esteemed music industry insiders, performers, and presenters.” This year’s show will be taped in Los Angeles and will premiere on Sunday, Nov. 26 at 8 p.m. ET/PT on BET and BET Her.

“Soul, R&B, and Hip Hop continue to top the charts and drive culture forward. This year, we are taking inspiration from these genres to innovate and disrupt the award show format while celebrating the legacy of Soul Train,” said Connie Orlando, EVP Specials, Music Programming & Music Strategy, in a statement. “We are proud of this year’s list of esteemed nominees and look to deliver an unforgettable night of Soul with our new location, performances, and show moments.”

The “Girls Need Love” singer is up for Best R&B/Soul Female Artist, Album of the Year, Song of the Year, Video of The Year, The Ashford and Simpson Songwriter’s Award, and twice for Best Dance Performance, as well as Best Collaboration.

Meanwhile, SZA is up for Best R&B/Soul Female Artist, Album of the Year, Video of the Year, Best Dance Performance, Best Collaboration, and twice for The Ashford and Simpson Songwriter’s Award, and Song of the Year. The King Of R&B is nominated for the Certified Soul Award, Best R&B/Soul Male Artist, Song of the Year, The Ashford and Simpson Songwriter’s Award, Best Collaboration, and twice for Best Dance Performance, and Video of the Year.

BET has not yet revealed who will receive the coveted Lady Of Soul or Legend Awards, nor have they unveiled this year’s list of performers. See the complete list of 2023 Soul Train Awards nominees below.
