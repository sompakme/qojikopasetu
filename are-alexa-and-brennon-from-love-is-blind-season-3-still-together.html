Are Alexa And Brennon From Love Is Blind Season 3 Still Together?

Since first airing in February 2020, Love Is Blind has become a go-to for many reality TV lovers, especially those who can't get enough of a fiery romantic tale. The show, of course, revolves around dating, in an attempt to create as many successful couples as possible by the end of the show. Singles are faced with the challenge of trying to find a match and fall in love. However, the catch is that those paired together have never seen each other face to face, forcing them to make a good judgment based on the emotional connection. This gives the contestants a chance to get to know each other on a deeper level before judging their physical appearance.

So far, there have been three seasons of the show that have all aired on Netflix, with the first airing in 2020. The show has proved to be incredibly popular, with the first season of the show pulling in over 30 million viewers, which is no mean feat. The third season, however, sparked even more interest after the only two marriages from season two imploded. Which begs the question, are fan-favorite married couple Alexa and Brennon still together today?

Alexa And Brennon Had A Successful Relationship On The Show

Season 3 of Love Is Blind officially premiered on October 19th, 2022. As usual, fans quickly developed their favorite couples, with some including Raven and SK, as well as Alexa and Brennon (of course). Both of these couples quickly gained popularity among fans due to the chemistry between them and how well they got along.

Alexa, who is the owner of an insurance agency, and Brennon, who is an engineer, paired up together and both appeared to get along incredibly well. Despite getting along so well, it appeared that the couple didn't quite agree on everything, especially in regard to parenting, where fans speculated Brennon seemed to display a bit of concern.

During later episodes of the season, it became abundantly clear to fans that the two shared quite big differences in terms of family background. The first example of this came when the two were discussing their parenting style together.

While Alexa tells Brennon that 'her family doesn't hold back', Brennon seems to express his concern: "With our kids, I'm definitely, like, Sit up straight for every meal. You know what I'm saying?". However, Alexa makes it clear that she doesn't understand at all. Awkward.

More family differences are later uncovered when the fan favorite couple finally meet each other's family and friends. While it is clear that Alexa comes from a more privileged background, Brennon comes from quite the opposite.

It seems that this hasn't prevented romance from blossoming between the two.

Are Alexa and Brennon From Love Is Blind Season 3 Still Together?

While the pair was clearly a fan favorite on the show, are they still together after the show has ended? While it may not be clear just from looking at social media, eagle-eyed fans finally got their answer during the reunion episode of the show.

During the reunion episode, the couple revealed that yes, they are in fact still together and going strong. Fans were also treated to an exclusive look into their blossoming relationship, learning that it hasn't been without its problems.

However, it seemed that they were able to work through them together, going on to explain how happy they both are within the relationship: “I’ve never been loved in the way that he loves me,” Alexa explained during the reunion episode.

In an interview with Extra in November 2022, the couple explained that they kept their relationship under the radar until they were both confident in their union.

Alexa also explained that they are always communicating with one another: "Like every few months, we’ll check in with each other, like, 'Do I need to do anything more as a wife, as a husband, as a partner?'"

However, Alexa and Brennon are not the only couple who have built a successful relationship outside the show. So, who else is still together?

There Were Other Successful Couples From Season 3 Of The Show

Besides Alexa and Brennon, there are other couples who have stayed together after the show. Colleen and Matt are still married, and often share loved-up snaps on social media for fans to see. The two even have plans to eventually move in together, however, no specific date was given.

Overall, that is two happy couples who have managed to build a successful marriage outside the show.

Despite not being able to make their relationships successful, the contestants still got paid for being on the show, meaning it wasn't a complete waste of time despite things not ending in marriage.
