José Bastón Net worth: All you need to know about Eva Longoria’s husband

Jose Baston is a media mogul in Mexico and the President of Grupo Televista which is one of the biggest media outfits in Latin America.

He is, however, popular known as the husband to actress Eva Longoria. This article is focused on what you should know about Jose Baston.

Profile Summary

Jose Baston’s Biography

The media mogul was born on the 13th of April, 1986, in Mexico City, Mexico, to Gloria Pation and Ricardo Alfredo Baston Talamantes; he lost his father in 2008. There is no indication of whether he has any siblings.

Education

Career

There are no details of his previous jobs before he joined Televista, where he was made the director of Grupo Televisa, S.A.B in 2011.

Relationships

Jose Baston has been in three relationships. He was married to actress Natalia Esperón for ten years, between 1995 to 2005.

After their split, he was reported to have been in a relationship with jewellery designer Paola Saad, but they break up over time.

Then in 2013, he and Eva Longoria romance began with reports revealing that they met through a mutual friend.

They managed to keep their relationship away from the media. After three years of dating, they married in May 2016 at Bastón’s home in Valle de Bravo, Mexico and are together till date.

Children

Jose Baston has four children; three from his ex-wife, Natalia Esperón, named Natalia, Mariana, and José Antonio. They also had a son named Sebastian, but he died a few days after his birth.

Jose and Eva have a son named Santiago Enrique, born in 2018.
