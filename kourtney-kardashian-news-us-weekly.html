Kourtney Kardashian News - Us Weekly

Name:Kourtney Kardashian

Hometown:Calabasas, California

Birthday:April 17, 1979

About

Kourtney Kardashian became famous as a result of Keeping Up With the Kardashians, the hit E! reality show starring her sister Kim Kardashian and more family members, which aired from 2007 to 2021.

Kourtney and her younger sister Khloé were featured on their own E! reality spinoff, Kourtney & Khloé Take Miami, in August 2009 when they opened a new location of their clothing store, Dash, in Miami. She starred in a second spinoff, Kourtney & Kim Take New York, from 2011 to 2012 to document the opening of their Dash store in New York City. Kourtney and her mother, Kris Jenner, also owned a children’s boutique called Smooch. She joined her family on Hulu’s The Kardashians in 2022.

Kardashian launched the lifestyle brand Poosh in 2019, covering topics such as health, wellness, motherhood, interior design and beauty. In collaboration with longtime friend Simon Huck, Kourtney unveiled Lemme — her line of all-natural vitamins and supplements — in September 2022.

Kourtney was involved in a lengthy on-off relationship with Scott Disick from 2006 to 2015. Kourtney gave birth to their first son, Mason, in 2009, three years before the pair welcomed daughter Penelope. In 2014, their son Reign was born.

Following her split from Disick, Kourtney dated model Younes Bendjima from 2016 to 2018.

She moved on with Travis Barker in early 2021 after years of friendship. The Blink-182 drummer popped the question on the beach in Montecito, California, the following October — which was all captured on The Kardashians. The couple had an unofficial wedding in Las Vegas in April 2022, legally tying the knot one month later at a California courthouse. They exchanged vows again in May 2022 in front of their friends and family in Italy.

Kourtney and Travis, who documented their IVF journey for Hulu cameras, announced in June 2023 that she was pregnant with their first baby together.
