WWE Partners With Roblox

WWE has announced a partnership with the game Roblox. Players can now play as characters that look like Roman Reigns, Xavier Woods, Becky Lynch and Seth Rollins. Check out the trailer below to see the characters in action.

Darby Allin Is A Free Agent

Darby Allin is now done with EVOLVE and WWN according to a report from PWInsider. The superstar became highly popular in the independent promotion and his underdog rise has been fun to watch. However, now he’s looking for a big move. Time will tell where he winds up at.
