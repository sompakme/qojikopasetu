What Happened To IShowSpeed? Death News Over The Internet And Health Update

Did Speed Get Shot Today? IShowSpeed Death Or Alive

Darren Watkins Jr., aka Ishowspeed, a famous young Youtuber and streamer, is not shot and is alive in his home. All the rumors are baseless and do not reflect any reality.

And Channel 46 News is just one in the bunch. Many media and publications have already covered his death news, stating he died this morning of a gunshot wound. But this is not true. He is not dead.

What Happened To IShowSpeed?
