Aaron Rodgers &amp; Danica Patrick Buy $28 Million Mansion In Malibu

Aaron Rodgers and Danica Patrick treated themselves nicely during the holiday season.

The well-known and popular power couple purchased a beautiful mansion in Malibu for the whopping price tag of $28 million, according to a report from James McClain of Variety.

Rodgers and Patrick bought the home from English singer Robbie Williams. The house measures 4,636 in square feet, and it includes four bathrooms and as many bedrooms. Williams and his family had previously bought it for $20.25 million, as pointed out by McClain.

The Green Bay Packers' star quarterback and the former NASCAR racer have been together since early 2018. Rodgers had previously dated Hollywood actress Olivia Munn for three years.

Patrick retired from racing in 2018 after participating in the Daytona 500 and Indianapolis 500. Her illustrious career saw Patrick win the Indianapolis 500 Rookie of the Year award in 2005, and she recorded seven top 10 finishes.

Ahead of the 2018 season, Rodgers agreed to a giant four-year deal worth $134 million with the Packers. Five years earlier, he signed a five-year extension that was worth $110 million. Throw in endorsements, and there's no doubting that he can certainly afford such a mansion along with his partner.

Rodgers and the Packers missed the postseason in 2017 and 2018 as he dealt with injuries. But Green Bay has already clinched the NFC North division, and they're in the driver's seat to secure the top seed in the NFC - meaning they need just two wins at Lambeau Field to get back to the Super Bowl.

The 36-year-old Rodgers led Green Bay to a Super Bowl XLV Championship nine years ago. The two-time MVP has also led them to the NFC Championship three times, though they lost their two most recent appearances - in 2014 and 2017.

But no matter what happens between now and the end of the season, Rodgers and Patrick will be able to enjoy life in a fancy and luxurious new home.
