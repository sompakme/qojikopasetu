Kenyan Drake Net Worth

Kenyan Drake is an American football running back and kick returner player with a net worth of $1.5 million.

Kenyan Drake signed a contract with the Miami Dolphins worth $3,381 million US dollars in June 2018, but he was also fined $12k for a fight a few months later.

His contract included an $821 thousand signing bonus as well as an $845 thousand annual salary.

His most recent contract was a two-year, $14.5 million contract with the Las Vegas Raiders.

Kenyan Drake was born on January 26, 1994. He is best known for his time as a running back for the Alabama Crimson Tide in college football.

As a student-athlete there, he won two national championships, the first in 2013 and the second in 2016.

Drake made his NFL debut in the season opener, a 12-10 loss to the Seattle Seahawks at CenturyLink Field. As a rookie, he appeared in all 16 games and rushed for 179 yards and two touchdowns.

In the 2020 season, Drake rushed for 60 yards and his first rushing touchdown of the season with The Cardinals.
