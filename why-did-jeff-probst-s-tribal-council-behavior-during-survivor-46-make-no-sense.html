Why did Jeff Probst's tribal council behavior during Survivor 46 make no sense?

Ever since Survivor 41, Jeff Probst has gone on and on about how players in the new era have to earn everything. As a result, Survivor players have to win extremely basic things like the flint they use to make fire. Then, Probst decides to start bringing licorice and popcorn to a couple of tribal councils to make a point about how lively they’ve been. While that was gimmicky on its face, the strange thing is that Probst shared the licorice and popcorn with the players. What happened to Survivor players having to earn everything?
