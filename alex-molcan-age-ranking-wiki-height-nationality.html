Alex Molcan Ranking

Alex has a career-high of world number 38 at the singles ranking and world number 268 at the doubles ranking.

He is now the No. 1 Slovak tennis player in singles.

He made his Australian Open debut in 2022 and clinched a major win over lucky loser Roman Safiullin in the first round.

At the 2021 Belgrade Open, in Morocco in 2022, as well as the 2022 ATP Lyon Open, Alex reached three ATP 250 finals.

At the ITF finals, he reached eleven singles with a record of 6 wins and 5 losses which includes a 2–2 result in ATP Challenger finals.
