Paula Deen's Weight Loss Encouraged Her to Join Dancing With the Stars

Sweating it out on the dance floor! Paula Deen is looking forward to shedding more pounds during her upcoming stint on Dancing With the Stars. The celebrity chef opened up about her goal during the show’s Season 21 casting announcement on Good Morning America on Wednesday, Sept. 2.

“It was a process getting here. But when I finally dropped the weight off I thought, ‘Well, maybe I can do it,'” Deen, 68, said. “Maybe I can do it now. And I’ve been eating out of my new cookbook that comes out next week.”

The Food Network star dropped 35 pounds back in March after being diagnosed with type 2 diabetes. Despite the accomplishment, she still won’t give up one item in her diet.

“I never gave up butter. Never, never,” she said. “But the big word in my life now is ‘moderation.’ Moderation, moderation, moderation.”
